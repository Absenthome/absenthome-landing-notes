# Welcome to Absenthome

I'm excited to launch **Absenthome**—a channel and community dedicated to three passions: **Salesforce development**, **home automation**, and **open source software**.

## What's This About?

Whether you're building enterprise solutions on Salesforce, automating your home with smart devices, or contributing to open source projects, this channel is for you. I create in-depth tutorials, practical guides, and project walkthroughs across all three domains.

### Salesforce Development
From Apex programming to Lightning Web Components, learn how to build scalable, maintainable applications on the Salesforce platform.

### Home Automation
Discover how to turn your house into a smart home. We'll explore popular platforms, DIY projects, and practical implementations.

### Open Source
Learn how to contribute to open source projects, build your own, and be part of the developer community.

## What to Expect

- **Weekly tutorials** covering real-world scenarios
- **Code-along videos** where you can follow along with projects
- **Deep dives** into complex topics
- **Community-driven content** based on your feedback
- **Comprehensive documentation** with all code samples and resources

## Get Started

1. **Watch** the latest videos on the [channel](https://youtube.com/@absenthome)
2. **Follow along** using the code samples and notes on this site
3. **Subscribe** to stay updated with new content
4. **Share** your projects and feedback

Thanks for joining this journey. Let's build something great together!

—Jessie
